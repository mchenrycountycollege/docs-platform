## Overview[](#Overview)

Connectors allow you to take advantage of third-party web services by creating a connection between these applications and Cascade CMS. Using connectors, you can publish Cascade CMS content to [WordPress](https://www.hannonhill.com/cascadecms/latest/cascade-administration/connectors/how-to-set-up-a-wordpress-connector.html) blogs, for example. You can also use connectors to generate [Google Analytics](https://www.hannonhill.com/cascadecms/latest/cascade-administration/connectors/how-to-set-up-a-google-analytics-connector.html) reports and embed [Spectate](https://www.hannonhill.com/cascadecms/latest/cascade-administration/connectors/how-to-set-up-a-spectate-connector.html)\-powered forms on pages.

Integrations such as for [Siteimprove](https://www.hannonhill.com/cascadecms/latest/cascade-administration/connectors/siteimprove-integration.html) and digital asset management (DAM) tools allow you to display or select data from external services for relevant content within Cascade CMS.
